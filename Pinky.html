Dear Ms. Pinky Villa
Greetings!

May this letter finds you in God's grace, peace, and good health.

We, the A Heart for Paws Gensan team, send this letter of goodwill and request your utmost generosity to keep our shelter in continuous operation. The AHFP shelter and works have been operating for three years now, and we could no longer tell how many times we are tempted to close down due to insufficient funding needed for daily operation. In spite of this difficulty and uncertainty, we know we can't just fold down. Each time we see strays on the street, their misery, their hunger, their emaciated body, their fight for life, we pause, pick our knees together, on our toes we go and save as many strays we could. Their resigned eyes say it all-the suffering they are forced to endure, every single day of their short lives. We resolved then, that AHFP continues with our crusade- save strays and provide them shelter. But this is only possible because we have compassionate generous people like you who travel with us on this journey. Once again, we ask for your help to keep our shelter afloat.

Currently, we have 19 rescues at the shelter, 10 fosters at 3 various foster parents, and upcoming rescues. We figured out, that at least we have a stable donation amounting to PHP 15,000 ( roughly USD 319.00 ) per month to assure the shelter's survival.

Here is a rough breakdown of our monthly expenses for the shelter:

  • P4,000 ( 81.45 USD )administrators salary.
  • P4,000 ( 81.45 USD ) caretaker salary.
  • P5,000 ( 101.81 USD) food of rescues and fosters.
  • P2,000 ( 40.72 USD) water, electricity, medicines.

Any amount you can pledge monthly to maintain our shelter will be very much appreciated. It will greatly impact our rescue and rehabilitation activities.

To help us maintain our shelter and continue taking care of our rescues until they are adopted to loving homes, kindly click on the button below, choose or input the amount you want to donate, select "Shelter Maintenance" on the dropdown arrow and tick the check box if you want it to be a monthly donation and another checkbox if you want to shoulder the processing fee too.
If you want another donation option, kindly click on the other button below. 

On behalf of all our rescues, soon to be rescued, and our team, our profound thanks for caring, and consistent support to AHFP.
Our eternal gratitude.
All for the good of our strays and rescues.
